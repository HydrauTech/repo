Who We Are

Hydrautech and Trucking Incorporated. founded by Mr.Genesis Malandac in 2023. started as a compact hydraulics service. Over the years, our team has grown by taking on different projects. Which helped us gain valuable experience in repairing and restoring hydraulic machines. Today, we are proud to be the main contractor for projects of all sizes.

We offer a wide range of services, incuding hydraulic system overhauls,diesel engine repairs. Marine vessel maintenance, salvaging, scrap and construction. Our specialities include winch restoration, vessel repairs high pressure re-piping, and precise engine installations. At Hydrautech and Trucking Inc. we are committed to delivering excellent results on every project.
